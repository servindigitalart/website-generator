# SEO Content Patterns for Medical Clinics

## Article structure for medical SEO

### Target: position 4-20 keywords (highest opportunity)
These are keywords where the clinic already has some visibility
but isn't converting to clicks. A good article can push
from position 12 → position 4, which multiplies clicks 10x.

### Article structure (1200-1800 words optimal)
1. Title (H1): includes keyword exactly once, benefit-driven
   Pattern: "[Keyword] in [City] — [Benefit]"
   Example: "Botox in Austin — What to Expect at Your First Visit"

2. Introduction (150 words):
   - Hook with patient problem/question
   - Establish clinic authority (board-certified, X years experience)
   - Preview what article covers

3. Main sections (H2s):
   - What is [treatment/condition]? (if educational)
   - Who is a good candidate?
   - What to expect at [Clinic Name]
   - Cost and insurance information
   - Frequently asked questions (H3s — excellent for featured snippets)

4. CTA section:
   - "Ready to book your [treatment] consultation?"
   - Button: "Schedule Today" or "Book Free Consultation"
   - Trust signals: board certification, patient count

5. FAQ schema (JSON-LD):
   - 4-6 questions as FAQ structured data
   - Answers 40-60 words each
   - Targets featured snippet and "People also ask" boxes

### Keyword patterns by specialty

Dermatology high-value clusters:
- "[treatment] [city]" (botox austin, laser austin)
- "best dermatologist [city]"
- "[condition] treatment [city]" (acne, eczema, psoriasis)
- "[treatment] cost [city]"

Orthopedics high-value clusters:
- "[procedure] surgeon [city]"
- "[body part] pain [city]"
- "knee/hip replacement [city]"
- "sports medicine doctor [city]"

Dental high-value clusters:
- "[procedure] dentist [city]"
- "emergency dentist [city]"
- "teeth whitening [city]"
- "invisalign [city]"
- "dental implants [city] cost"

Med Spa high-value clusters:
- "[treatment] [city]"
- "best med spa [city]"
- "[treatment] near me"
- "[treatment] cost [city]"

### Schema markup per article
Always include:
- Article schema (author, datePublished, publisher)
- FAQPage schema (for question sections)
- MedicalOrganization schema on every page
- BreadcrumbList for navigation context

### Internal linking strategy
Each new article should link to:
- The relevant service page
- The doctor profile page (builds E-E-A-T)
- The contact/booking page (conversion)
- 1-2 other recent articles (link equity distribution)
