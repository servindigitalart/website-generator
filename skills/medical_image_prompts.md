# Medical Clinic Image Prompts — by Specialty and Section

## Prompt engineering principles for medical imagery
- Always: photorealistic, professional photography style
- Always: high resolution, sharp focus, natural lighting
- Always: diverse, inclusive representation
- Never: stock-photo-obvious, overly posed, generic
- Never: medical equipment as hero (patients are the hero)
- Aspect ratios: hero 16:9, team portrait 3:4, service card 4:3

## Prompt structure template
"{subject}, {setting}, {lighting}, {mood}, {photography style},
 {technical specs}"
